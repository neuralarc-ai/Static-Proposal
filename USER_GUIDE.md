## üë®‚Äçüíº Admin Portal Guide

### Dashboard Overview

After logging in as admin, you will see:
- **Statistics Cards** - Total partners, active proposals, revenue, conversion rate
- **Recent Partners** - Latest partner additions
- **Recent Proposals** - Latest proposals from all partners
- **Quick Actions** - Fast access to common tasks

### Managing Partners

#### Adding a New Partner

1. Navigate to **Partners** section from the sidebar
2. Click the **"Add Partner"** button (top right)
3. Fill in the partner information:
   - **Partner Name** - Full name of the partner
   - **Company Name** - Their company or organization
   - **Email Address** - Contact email
   - **4-Digit PIN** - Create a unique PIN (e.g., 1234)
4. Click **"Save Partner"** to create the account

#### Editing a Partner

1. Find the partner card in the Partners section
2. Click the **"Edit"** button on their card
3. Update the information as needed
4. Save changes

#### Deleting a Partner

1. Find the partner card in the Partners section
2. Click the **"Delete"** button (red)
3. Confirm the deletion
4. The partner and their data will be removed

### Managing Price Lists

#### Creating a Price List

1. Navigate to **Price Lists** section from the sidebar
2. Click **"Create Price List"** button
3. Select the partner from the dropdown
4. Choose the currency (USD, INR, EUR, GBP)
5. Enter pricing for each item:
   - **Helium License (Monthly)** - Monthly subscription cost
   - **Helium License (Annual)** - Annual subscription cost
   - **Development Rate (Per Hour)** - Hourly development rate
   - **Deployment Cost (One-time)** - Initial deployment fee
   - **Maintenance Cost (Monthly)** - Monthly maintenance fee
6. Click **"Save Price List"**

#### Editing a Price List

1. Find the partner's price list card
2. Click the **"Edit"** button
3. Update the pricing values
4. Save changes

**Important:** Each partner has their own personalized price list. This allows you to offer different rates to different partners based on your business relationships.

### Viewing Proposals

#### Proposals Overview

1. Navigate to **Proposals** section from the sidebar
2. You will see proposals organized by partner
3. Use the filter tabs to view:
   - All Proposals
   - Pending (awaiting approval)
   - Approved (accepted by clients)
   - Draft (work in progress)

#### Viewing Proposal Details

1. Click on any proposal card
2. You will see the complete proposal document with:
   - Executive summary
   - Project scope and features
   - Timeline breakdown
   - Investment breakdown
   - Deliverables
   - Terms and conditions

## ü§ù Partner Portal Guide

### Partner Dashboard

After logging in as a partner, you will see:
- **Chat Interface** - AI-powered proposal generation
- **Previous Proposals** - Thumbnail grid of your proposals

### Creating a Proposal

#### Step 1: Describe Your Project

1. In the chat interface, type your project requirements
2. Include details such as:
   - Project objectives and goals
   - Key features needed
   - Timeline expectations
   - Technology preferences
   - Team size requirements
   - Budget considerations

**Example:**
```
I need to build an e-commerce platform for a retail client. 
The platform should include:
- Product catalog with 500+ items
- Shopping cart and checkout
- Payment gateway integration
- Customer accounts
- Admin dashboard
- Mobile responsive design
Timeline: 6 months
```

#### Step 2: AI Analysis

1. Click **"Send"** to submit your requirements
2. The AI will analyze your input
3. It will ask clarifying questions if needed
4. Once ready, it will offer to generate a proposal

#### Step 3: Generate Proposal

1. Click **"Generate Proposal"** button
2. The system will create a detailed proposal using your custom pricing
3. You will be redirected to the proposal view

### Viewing Your Proposals

1. Previous proposals appear as cards below the chat interface
2. Each card shows:
   - Proposal title
   - Client name
   - Creation date
   - Timeline
   - Total value
   - Status badge
3. Click any card to view the full proposal

### Exporting Proposals

#### Export as PDF

1. Open any proposal
2. Click **"Export as PDF"** button
3. Your browser's print dialog will open
4. Select "Save as PDF" as the destination
5. Choose location and save

**PDF Features:**
- Professional formatting
- Company branding
- Proper page breaks
- Print-optimized layout

### Requesting More Information

If you need clarification or additional details:

1. Open the proposal you have questions about
2. Click **"Request More Info"** button
3. A dialog box will appear
4. Type your question or request
5. Click **"Send Request"**
6. Your message will be emailed to the admin team
7. They will respond via email within 24 hours

## üîç Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Cannot Login:**
- Verify you are entering the correct 4-digit PIN
- Ensure you have selected the correct role (Partner/Admin)
- Check that all 4 digits are entered
- Contact admin if PIN is forgotten

**Proposal Not Generating:**
- Ensure you have provided sufficient project details
- Check that your price list is configured
- Try refreshing the page
- Contact support if issue persists

**PDF Export Issues:**
- Use Chrome or Firefox for best results
- Ensure pop-ups are not blocked
- Check printer settings in browser
- Try "Save as PDF" instead of printing

**Modal Not Closing:**
- Click the X button in the top right
- Click outside the modal area
- Press the Escape key
- Refresh the page if needed
